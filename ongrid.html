On-Grid Solar System - Smart Energy for a Brighter Future

An On-Grid Solar System (also known as a Grid-Tied Solar System) is one of the most cost-effective and efficient ways to harness solar energy. It is directly connected to the public electricity grid and does not require battery storage, making it ideal for areas with a reliable power supply.

Key Features:

Direct Grid Connection: Uses solar energy during the day and automatically draws power from the grid when needed.

Net Metering: Excess energy generated is exported back to the grid, allowing you to earn credits or reduce your electricity bill.

Low Maintenance: With no batteries involved, system maintenance is minimal.

High Efficiency: Maximizes the use of sunlight during the day for immediate consumption and cost savings.
