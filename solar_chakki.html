Solar Chakki (Solar Flour Mill)- Empowering Rural Communities with Clean Energy

The Solar Chakki is an innovative solar-powered flour mill designed to grind grains efficiently without relying on grid electricity or diesel. Powered entirely by solar energy, it is an ideal solution for villages, small farmers, self-help groups (SHGs), and rural entrepreneurs, promoting self-reliance and sustainable livelihoods.

Key Features:

Runs Entirely on Solar Power – No need for diesel or electricity.

Efficient Grain Grinding – Suitable for wheat, rice, maize, millet, and more.

Portable & Easy to Operate – User-friendly design with minimal maintenance.

Battery Backup (Optional) – Operates even in low sunlight or at night.

Low Operational Cost – Zero fuel and low maintenance requirements.
